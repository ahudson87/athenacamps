Athena Camps are for girls entering kindergarten through 8th grade. Every camp combines athletic, creative, emotional, and social activities to help girls grow in confidence and courage.

Camp Descriptions

  • athena camp mini

    Grades K-2

    • Sports

      Girls are split into small groups for two 40 minutes sessions of high energy, fun, sport-specific games and activities - with lots of water breaks. Sport-specific equipment is modified for your girls. Instruction is energetic and brief. Girls enjoy multiple cooperative games and daily successes.

      Team Time is a group experience where girls are introduced to the concepts of teamwork and teammates with a dedicated coach for 30 minutes each day. Physical skills learned during sport time are reinforced as well during Team Time.

        Choose from:
      • Tennis & Volleyball
      • Soccer & Basketball
      • Yoga & Running

    • Activities

      We emphasize group activities and games that teach interdependence in light fun ways.

      Daily Opening, Affirmation, and Closing Circles set a positive environment while Girl Power Games bring girls together to play and connect.

      A special Athena Camp "Boost Board" invites girls to leave positive messages for each other all day, every day. As girls open up and make new friends, the notes multiply.

      Art projects & Girl talk invite girls to create unique art projects based on the weekly theme, allowing creative freedom and expression. Art coaches always encourage girls to share their special project with the group in a supportive setting.

      Friday afternoons are our special Family Closing Circles.

    • 2015 Camp Themes

      1 - Healthy Girls = Happy Girls

      With the crazy amount of activities and expectations placed on our girls today, it’s easy for their lives to spiral into an unhealthy place. At Athena Camps we explore the many aspects of girls’ lives that contribute to their health. The week is filled with creative projects, tools and talks designed to promote health of the whole girl and give her a sense of respect, love for her body, and control over her own wellness.

      2 - Acts of Kindness

      The value of helping others is something we all hope our daughters possess when they leave the nest. Spend a week with us as we have a blast exploring fun & meaningful ways girls can give. We will explore the positives as girls learn to give as well as the art and importance of saying no.

      3 - Friends Rock!

      Friendships rule a girl’s world. Navigating the social scene at any age can be filled with confusion and challenges. At Athena Camps we are all about fun as our coaches deliver lively activities and discussions designed to help girls wade through the rough waters of relationship dilemmas particular to their age.

  • athena camp jr

    Grades 3-5

    • Sports

      Girls are divided into age-appropriate groups for two 55-minute stations each day. Sport-specific techniques, drills, and games are taught daily with each lesson building on the previous one. By the end of each week, girls learn all the main skills necessary to play each sport. Sport-specific equipment is modified for age and ability. Each sport lesson includes fun, cooperative games.
