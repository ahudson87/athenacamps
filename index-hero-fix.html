All Girls. All Day.

Discover a camp where creativity thrives, where girls are encouraged to color outside the lines, where they cooperate rather than compete, and where they lift each other up. Welcome to Athena Camps.

Why you donâ€™t want to miss out on camp:

At Athena Camps, we create unique experiences that support girls in building friendships, learning sports, expressing creativity, and having girl-powered fun.

  • Sports Our sports are about skill building, personal growth, and teamwork. Each girl gains positive body image, improves self-esteem, and learns life lessons through sports while building body confidence.
  • Creative We nurture an environment that promotes imagination, discovery, and self-expression. Every day, girls use their unique creative abilities on different art projects to build creative confidence.
  • Social & Emotional We create special place where girls are offered tools to connect, support, and nurture one another in meaningful ways. They learn to respect one another, celebrate friendship, and build social confidence.
  • Exceptional Staff Our coaches are strong, inspirational women passionate about bringing out the best in girls and each other. Girls receive affirmation bracelets and play with a team of positive female role models.
